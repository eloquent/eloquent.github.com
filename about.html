Eloquent

Eloquent Software is an organisation formed to produce high-quality software projects. Projects that stand on their own merits, fulfill a purpose, and express themselves with eloquence; that is, with simplicity, style, and power.

Eloquent is primarily the brain child of myself (Erin Millard), an Australian software developer, currently living in Brisbane.
